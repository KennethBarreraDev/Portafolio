Tu taxi Morelia

"Tu taxi Morelia" is a mobile application that allows the user to request a taxi service to move between two points of their city

aptitudes:
Flutter
Firebase
Javascript
Node js
Mobile developing
Web developing

On his main screen, the driver has a map where he can see his current location as well as a button with which he can end his work shift.

The driver receives real-time travel notifications even if he is outside the app

Before requesting a trip, the client can view the route to their destination as well as the price of the service

Once the travel has started, the user can view the route being followed

The user can communicate via chat with his driver throughout the service

Both, user and driver have a 'panic button', through which they can report the occurrence of an accident or dangerous situation.

Once the trip has finished, the user can evaluate his driver and the service he offered
